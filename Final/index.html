Rock Runway

Shred Your Way to Fame

Project Documentation

Overview

Welcome to Rock Runway, where music meets mayhem.

In this vertical-scrolling action game, you play as a purple-haired rockstar sprinting down the red carpet to fame. Dodge bad contracts and critics’ noise as you shred your way forward. Blast the right notes with your guitar and play your way into the spotlight.



Controls

Arrow keys - Move

Spacebar - Blast



Instructions

- Dodge bad contracts and critics’ noise, but blast the music notes!
- You lose a life each time you hit an obstacle or miss a note.
- Earn an extra life for every 10 notes you hit.
- Blast 60 notes to reach the top!
